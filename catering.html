Catering & Private Events

For private events of 15 or more people please contact us via the form below or at z23ma@uwaterloo.ca.

Whether you’re planning a cozy family gathering or a larger celebration, Main St. Local Kitchen offers flexible catering options built around our seasonal menu.

We’re happy to accommodate vegan guests, gluten-friendly options, and little foodies with smaller portions, so everyone at your table feels welcome.

To start planning your event, please reach out to us by email or phone, and we’ll work with you to create a menu that fits your occasion.
